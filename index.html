About the Event

Cozy Cove Aquarium is opening soon, and you're invited! Our brand-new aquarium offers an up-close look into the lives of fascinating marine animals. This event will feature tours for our exhibits, and fun activities for guests of all ages.

Event Schedule

Event Activity 1 - Meet Up 11:30AM

Our guests will be welcomed by the tour guides at the main entrance at the front of the building. Here they will receive name tags, brochures, and a rundown of the day's activities. This is a great time to get to know your fellow visitors before the tour officially begins!

Event Activity 2 - Aquarium Walkthrough 11:30AM

This portion of the tour takes you through the layout of the aquarium. You will by guided through each section, learning the themes and special features of different areas like our coral reef habitats and interactive touch tanks. We hope this will help you get an overview of where our exhibits are.

Event Activity 3 - Exhibits 12:30PM

Here's where the real magic happens! Guests will spend time at the exhibits, watching a variety of learning life in action. Informational talks by staff and short feeding sessions will also be included during this time!

Event Activity 4 - Lunch 2:00PM

We will be providing meals in our aquarium's cafe. Our options include favorites such as fish-shaped grilled cheese sandwiches, chicken tenders, pizzas, salad wraps, and more!

Event Activity 5 - Activities 3:30PM

To end off the day, we will be hosting hands-on activities to turn learning into action! These sessions are filled with educational games, crafts, and conservation-themed activities. Kids and adults alike will be able to make ocean-theme crafts, and learn how to protect marine life and reduce ocean pollution.
